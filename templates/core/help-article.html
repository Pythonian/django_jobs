{% trans 'How to create an Employee account' %}

{% trans 'Account' %}

In today's fast-paced job market, finding the right candidates for non-professional jobs can be a challenging task for both employers and job seekers. This is where Blue Jobs comes in. We are a recruitment management platform that connects employers with job seekers for non-professional jobs in a simple, efficient, and cost-effective manner.

Our mission at Blue Jobs is to revolutionize the way non-professional jobs are recruited and managed. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to job opportunities, regardless of their level of experience or education. That's why we have created a platform that is accessible to both employers and job seekers, and that provides a hassle-free way to fill and find non-professional jobs.

Blue Jobs is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the information that we collect, how we use it, and the steps that we take to ensure that your personal information is kept secure. Information Collection and Use At Blue Jobs, we collect and use personal information to provide you with the best possible user experience. The information that we collect may include your name, email address, telephone number, employment history, educational background, and job preferences. We may also collect information about your use of our platform, such as the pages that you visit and the job listings that you apply for. This information is used to understand how our platform is being used and to improve our services. Information Sharing and Disclosure Blue Jobs does not share or sell your personal information to third parties, except as outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, payment processing, and data analysis. These third-party service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are only allowed to use your information to provide the services that they are contracted to perform. We may also disclose your personal information if required by law or if we believe that it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Blue Jobs or its users. Security At Blue Jobs, we take the security of your personal information very seriously. We use industry-standard security technologies, such as encryption and firewalls, to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Despite our best efforts, no security system is 100% secure. If you have reason to believe that your personal information has been compromised, please contact us immediately. At Blue Jobs, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it. Information Collection We collect personal information from our users when they create an account on our platform, post job listings, or apply for jobs. This information may include, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, telephone numbers, job preferences, and work experience. We may also collect information about users' usage of our platform, such as the types of jobs they search for, the pages they visit, and the actions they take. This information is used to improve our platform and provide better services to our users. Use of Personal Information We use personal information for the following purposes: 1. To provide our users with the services they need to find and fill non-professional jobs. 2. To communicate with our users about their accounts, job applications, and other related matters. 3. To send users notifications about new job listings, updates, and other relevant information. 4. To improve our platform and develop new features that better meet the needs of our users. We will not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Data Security We use the latest encryption and security technologies to protect the personal information we collect. Our platform is designed to meet the highest standards of data security and is regularly audited by independent security experts. Access to Personal Information Users have the right to access and update their personal information at any time. They can do this by logging into their account and updating their profile information. If a user would like to delete their account, they can contact us and we will remove all personal information from our systems. Changes to Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements. We will notify our users of any changes by posting an updated version on our platform. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at privacy@bluejobs.com. We will be happy to assist you.
